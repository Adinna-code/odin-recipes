Air Fryer Buffalo Chicken Wings from Frozen

Description

Forget to thaw those wings and in a pinch? No problem. Here is my how-to cook chicken wings from frozen to the table in under 40 minutes with only two ingredients.

Ingradients

  • 1 pound frozen, ready-to-cook chicken wings
  • Â½ (8 ounce) bottle Buffalo wing sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Place wings in the air fryer basket.
  3. Cook wings for 15 minutes, turn, and cook for 15 more minutes. Meanwhile, place wing sauce in a small saucepan and heat on low heat.
  4. Flip wings one more time and cook until crispy, about 6 more minutes.
  5. Transfer wings to a large bowl and pour warmed Buffalo wing sauce over the top. Toss and serve immediately.

Happy eating!
